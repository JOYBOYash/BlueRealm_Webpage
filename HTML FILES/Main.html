Tears of Ocean

Dive into an educational and entertaining gaming experience with our project centered on SDG 14: Life Below Water. Encounter the challenges facing aquatic life, from pollution to habitat destruction, through engaging gameplay and interactive decision-making. This game not only entertains but also empowers players to make informed choices, promoting real-world action in support of marine conservation. With stunning visuals and a commitment to positive change, our game aims to inspire a generation to actively contribute to the protection of life below water.
