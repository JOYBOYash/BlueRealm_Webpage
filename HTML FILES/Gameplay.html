Gameplay

Mechanics

Primary Objective - To clean ocean by collecting plastic and garbage in ocean floor.

Encounter & Destroy polluting drones that attempt to further degrade the underwater environment.

Fast paced Third Person Shooter game that implements a strategic view on SDG 14.

Energy System

The stamina bar is crucial for sustaining your actions and progression in the game.

Actions like boosting forward deplete stamina, while gathering garbage restores it.

Ensure strategic plays from players and promotes healthy gameplay.

Stunning Visuals

Immerse yourself in a visually striking deep ocean environment with realistic details.

Experience the deep ocean through real sounds, enhancing the game's immersive qualities.

Engaging Elements: Graphics and sound contribute to filling action and satisfying experience.

SDG-14 Prominance

Bridging Reality and Fiction: BlueRealm encourages players to reflect on the real-world consequences of marine pollution.

Conservation Advocacy: The game serves as a platform to raise awareness about ocean conservation.

Develops player mentality to preserve beutiful aquatic life through it's immersive gameplay.
